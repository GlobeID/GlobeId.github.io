what is GlobeID?

GlobeID is a Non-Profit Organization Founded to provide simple tool's to World Conquest Players. We pla to expand our tool category and game's applicable soon.

How do I submit a flag?

To submit a flag head over to our DISCORD, Join the server and submit a flag on #flag-upload. It will be added to the website when you recieve the check mark on your discord flag post.

How to use the searchbar?

First ensure you are on the FLAG page, go to the searchbar and you can enter the following terms: A location like Europe, a creator like UnitedCommunities#1234, Flag Names and even Genres are coming soon to help improve your experience searching the catalog.

How do I use a flag I found?

When you find a flag there will be a textbox at the bottom of the card that displays a list of numbers, if you put this list of numbers into the Custom ID box on the World Conquest flag page you will be able to use that custom Flag.
