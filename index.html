How does this site work?

This site uses community made flags and puts them in a library of flags where you can search through genres, creators and nations to find the right flag for your custom nation.

The label that is colored just below the flag shows the creator. Pink means that this flag (using the scenario/description given) can provide a real roleplay background. Purple means that this is a utility to make flags or textures and is not a flag (A Texture) Cyan means that the background provided is likely voidanle inside of the game.

What do I do once I found a flag?

Once you find a flag you want either through search or scrollin then you want to copy the id of the flga (located inside of the purple textbox at the bottom of the card), if you are on mobile then you want to press "copy phone number" as it thinks it is a phone number. You then want to join the game you want and under the customization section their should be Custom ID box, in there paste the id given and press enter. You should now have the wanted flag.
